> **__Helpful Commands__**
.tag:intro
**__Introduction__**
This channel contains a list of all Clue Chasers discord commands along with a brief summary of what they contain. Feel free to use any of the commands in the <#333343575737892864> channel.

.
> __**Information Hub**__
.tag:infohub
⬥ `!bik` - Explains the Scripture of Bik.
⬥ `!clue` - Explains what a Clue Scroll is.
⬥ `!cluehelp` - Provides a link to the Clue Assistance channels.
⬥ `!cannon` - Shows an image of cannon positioning for each clue gathering.
⬥ `!facet` - Explains the Dark Facet of Luck.
⬥ `!kph` - Gives you a breakdown of kph for each clue gathering method.
⬥ `!nav` - Explains navigation inside the Clue Chasers Discord.
⬥ `!sbs` - Explains Spellbook Swapping and it's utility.
⬥ `!sotg` - Explains Sceptre of The Gods and it's utility.
⬥ `!totem` - Explains the Totem of Treasure and it's utility.

.
> __**Server Help**__
.tag:serverhelp
⬥ `!nav` - Lists some ways of navigating Clue Chasers.
⬥ `!notrading` - Shows our server rules and explains that trading is not allowed in our channels.
⬥ `!clue` - Generates an invite link for Clue Chasers.
⬥ `!cluehelp` - Links the Clue help forum and questions channel.
⬥ `!suggestion` - Links suggestions forum and offers examples of possible suggestions.
⬥ `!website` - Links the Clue Chasers website.
⬥ `!twitter` - Links the Clue Chasers twitter.

.
> __**Globetrotter Outfit & Gear**__
.tag:outfit
⬥ `!gorder` - Lists the priority order and which pieces should be prioritized based on tier. 
⬥ `!gjacket` - Explains the globetrotter jacket and it's functionality.
⬥ `!gbackpack` - Explains the globetrotter backpack and it's functionality.
⬥ `!garms` - Explains the globetrotter arm guards and it's functionality.
⬥ `!gshorts` - Explains the globetrotter shorts and it's functionality.
⬥ `!gboots` - Explains the globetrotter boots and it's functionality.
.
> __**Collection Log**__
.tag:rewards
⬥ `!fortunates` - Generates a list of possible items that could be disassembled into fortunate components.
⬥ `!fortunates` - Generates a list of every item that can be created with the necessary ingredients required.

.
> __**Abilities/Spells**__
.tag:spells
⬥ `!Dive` - Explains Dive ability and it's functionality.
⬥ `!Barge` - Explains Barge ability and it's functionality.
⬥ `!Escape` - Explains Escape ability and it's functionality.
⬥ `!Surge` - Explains Surge ability and it's functionality.

.
> __**Spreadsheets**__
.tag:sheets
⬥ `!casketvalue` - Generates a list of the average value of each tier of clue casket.
⬥ `!hof` - Generates a link to Hall of Fame spreadsheet, a place dedicated to our most prestigious members.
⬥ `!most3a` - List players with the most Third Age Dyes on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostblood` - List players with the most Blood Dyes on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostbarrows` - List players with the most Barrows Dyes on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostshadow` - List players with the most Shadow Dyes on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostice` - List players with the most Ice Dyes on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostdruidic` - List players with the most total of Druidic Pieces on their collection logs.
⬥ `!mostosh` - List players with the most Orlando Smith Hat's on their collection logs.
