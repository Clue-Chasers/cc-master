> __**Alt1 Overview**__
.tag:alt1
⬥ Alt1 is a 3rd-party program that consists of several application overlays on your screen.
⬥ They get information by reading your screen, and provide a bunch of QoL/useful features.

.
> __**How It Works**__
⬥ Alt1 is designed to capture images of the NXT Client and parse them with an OCR (Optical Character Recognition) Library.
⬥ The captured data is then transformed into pixel data that can be used in Alt1 apps.
⬥ More information can be found on the forums and in the Alt1 Toolkit discord.

.
> __**Is It Safe?**__
⬥ Alt1 is essentially a glorified Chromium browser that creates overlays over your game. Every Alt1 app is essentially a webpage.
⬥ Alt1 does not send any inputs or otherwise directly interact with your client beyond reading the pixels on the screen.
⬥ **It is not a client, therefore you won't get banned for using it.** https://www.reddit.com/r/runescape/comments/3ovux7/the_alt1_toolkit_has_been_reviewed_by_jagex_and/

.
> __**Clue Solver**__
.tag:cluesolver
⬥ This is a very useful app that looks at your currently open clue on screen, and tells you how to solve it.
⬥ It handles every type of clue except for scans which are explained through the <#1123544061052207114>
⬥ Have your clue visible on-screen clearly and click the 'Unlock Any' button to find a solution to your clue that will be displayed on the overlay.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/EhTPOsI.png
.
> __**3rd-Party Alt1 Apps**__
.tag:apps
In addition to the default apps that come with Alt1, there are a number of other useful third-party apps that can be installed.

⬥ Alt1 Addons can be installed by going to the correct URL in either your normal browser or the Alt1 in-built browser.
⬥ This will bring up a prompt to add the application to Alt1.
⬥ The list of apps below have the full URL following each one.
⬥ Note that none of these links will work on mobile, as they require Alt1 to be installed.

.
> __**Scan Trainer**__ - `alt1://addapp/https://leridon.github.io/cluetrainer-live/appconfig.json`
.tag:scantrainer
**Explanation** - guides players through efficiently solving scan steps in elite <:eliteclue:1123586941057769552> and master clues <:masterclue:1123586937245151303> see <#1123544061052207114>

.
> __**Bik Book Timer**__ - `alt1://addapp/https://amerryma.github.io/alt1-bik-proc-timer/appconfig.json`
.tag:bikbook
**Explanation** - alerts when to disassemble with Bik book <:scriptureofbik:892342109791735848>

.
> __**OpenLogger**__ - `alt1://addapp/https://redx1000.github.io/OpenLogger/appconfig.json`
.tag:openlogger
**Explanation** - an open source clue logger that will help keep track of your clues as you open them

.
> __**Clue Splits**__ - `alt1://addapp/https://yongyi781.github.io/clue-split/appconfig.json`
.tag:splits
**Explanation** - automatically tracks your clue times while doing a clue run, with no input required <:surge:535533810004262912>

.
> __**Catalyst Tracker**__ - `alt1://addapp/https://yongyi781.github.io/clue-split/appconfig.json`
.tag:catalyst
**Explanation** - tracks clues from Catalyst of alterations displayed in the chat window

This following is a guide to be used in conjunction with <@420315520449904640> <#1120257028762378310> for those looking to earn the full Globetrotter outfit but are more advanced in terms of their account. This will also include some extra cost over a Beginner preset, but not the maximum cost (e.g. 1 Passage of the Abyss is used instead of 2) and will assume you have all quests and all achievement diaries complete.
If you opt to use 2 Passage of the Abyss then follow this guide <#1022566907645263892> for your teleports⁠.
This particular resource is meant to show you the semi-advanced presets for Hards, Elites and Masters.
.
For how to use Alt1s Clue Solver to solve clues, please check <#1120257028762378310>


In order to efficiently farm Treasure Trail points for full Globetrotter <:globetrotterjacket:1123592261175889971> this is the amount of clues you will need to complete:
.
<:hardclue:1123586945273049109> 1000 Hard Clues
<:hardclue:1123586945273049109> 750 Hard Clues + <:masterclue:1123586937245151303> 69 Master Clues
<:hardclue:1123586945273049109> 500 Hard Clues + <:eliteclue:1123586941057769552> 120 Elite Clues + <:masterclue:1123586937245151303> 74 Master Clues
<:eliteclue:1123586941057769552> 500 Elite Clues
<:eliteclue:1123586941057769552> 350 Elite Clues + <:masterclue:1123586937245151303> 93 Master Clues
*Note: All Master numbers are assuming the average drop rate of Masters from caskets. If you have masters to start with, do those first and then use the following calculator to find out what you need to do afterwards:*
<https://runescape.wiki/w/Calculator:Other/Treasure_Trails_reward_points>

> **__Why/why not a certain Clue tier?__**
.tag:tiers
⬥ <:easyclue:1123586950989885510> Easy clue scrolls offer very little Treasure Trail points and are not worth doing to obtain the outfit.

⬥ <:mediumclue:1123586948905324637> Medium clue scrolls require the Way of the Foot Shaped Key reward to be efficient and this is not worth purchasing prior to the outfit.

⬥ <:masterclue:1123586937245151303> Master clue scrolls are an incredible source of Treasure Trail points and should be done as much as possible due to their speed vs reward point potential.
    • Costume skipping tickets <:costumeskipticket:1123590734507954215> can be used to avoid needing any of the hard to get items. The average reward of a Master outweighs the cost of a Costume skipping ticket <:costumeskipticket:1123590734507954215> by a huge margin.
.
> **__Which is the fastest method?__**
.tag:fastest
This entirely depends on how fast you solve each tier of clue.
You can use this sheet to find out the points per hour for each tier based on your own solve speed: <#1089570205216288889>
*Note: Master drop rate (after re-rolls) from Hards is 1/11.25 and from Elites it's 1/3.75. So even if Hards may seem better points per hour vs Elites, take the amount of Masters you get into account as well.*
.
