### **__[Max Cape](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Max_cape>)__** <:maxcape:580177795703898117>
⬥ Offers three perk slots that can be filled with [Capes of Accomplishment](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Capes_of_Accomplishment>) by using them on it, as well as the functionality to view and remove existing perks.
⬥ Max cape perks apply to backpack allowing you to utilize the [globetrotter backpack](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Globetrotter_backpack>) <:globetrotterbackpack:1123592248056102993>, [Dungeoneering Cape](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Dungeoneering_cape>) <:dgcape:976732922545586246>, [Slayer Cape](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_cape>) <:slayercape:975284344123953164> [Quest Cape](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Quest_Cape>) <:questcape:1123599570832736287> all at once.
### **__[Archaeology](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Archaeology>)__** <:archaeology:941211288665739274>
⬥ [Abyssal Link](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Abyssal_Link>) <:abyssallink:1124762426265583727> requires level 112. (can be boosted)
⬥ [Shadow's Grace](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Shadow's_Grace>) <:Shadows_Grace:1128078767399653486> requires level 67. (can be boosted)
⬥ [Persistent Rage](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Persistent_Rage>) <:persistentrage:1128437146546622504> requires level 98. (can be boosted)
### **__[Herblore](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Herblore>)__** <:herblore:689554435583508558>
⬥ [Adrenaline Renewals](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Adrenaline_renewal_flask>) <:adrenrenewalflask:736298313980182541> requires level 115. (can be boosted) (if used in the preset)

> ## **__Other__**

### **__[Full Globetrotter outfit](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Globetrotter_outfit>)__** <:globetrotterjacket:1123592261175889971>
⬥ The globetrotter outfit is an outfit that is obtainable from the [Treasure Trails Reward Shop](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Treasure_Trails_Reward_Shop>) with points obtained by completing [Treasure Trails](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Treasure_Trails>).
        ⬩ The outfit will provide several QoL features some of which are charged-based through solving clues.
.
### **__[Double Surge ability](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Double_Surge_codex>)__** <:surgecodex:602561894414417944>
⬥ An item that can be read to unlock an upgrade to the [Surge](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Surge>) ability, giving it an extra charge with a separate independent and hidden cooldown.
        ⬩ The double Surge ability is not functional in the [Wilderness](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Wilderness>) when opted into PvP.
        ⬩ It can be created with 750 [codex pages](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Codex_page>) using the lectern. It is consumed upon use.
        ⬩ This version can be bought from or sold to other players or the Grand Exchange, but the __untradeable version only requires 500 codex pages__.

.
### **__[Sceptre of the Gods](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Sceptre_of_the_gods>)__** <:sotg:1123599971023847485>
⬥ The Sceptre of the gods is an item that may be received when playing [Pyramid Plunder](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Pyramid_Plunder>).
⬥ It teleports to the same locations as the regular [pharaoh's sceptre](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Pharaohs_sceptre>) <:pharaohsceptre:1123599968477909052> but has an additional teleport to the Golden Palace in Menaphos.
⬥ The sceptre has 10 teleport charges when fully charged, which is increased to 20 with [medium Desert achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Desert_achievements#Medium_achievements>) completed, and further to 60 with [hard Desert achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Hard_Desert_achievements>) completed.
.
