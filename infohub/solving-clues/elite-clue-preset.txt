> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
The below presets will cover both a non-skip ticket and skip ticket setup, as well as an alternative preset for when your Sentinel Outfit runs out/if you dont want to use the Sentinel Outfit. Presets also assume you are using <:Bik:908044805475553321> whilst solving. If you aren't, replace <:Bik:908044805475553321> and <:Spiritattractionflask:1127681167005589545> with items of your choice.
To see the cost analysis of no skips vs. using skips, use the command !casket in <#333343575737892864>.
Any questions regarding the presets, ask in <#400499160094212097>.
Improvements and suggestions are always welcome. Post any you may have in <#874985149937365064>

> __**Tips & Tricks**__
.tag:tricks
In-game sounds are able to assist with scans. Listening to the pulse instead of staring at your feet allows you focus on surroundings and get information as you are teleporting from one place to another.

⬥ Use Totem of Summoning on Anachronia for an extended meerkat timer <:totemoftreasure:1124764867237269665>
⬥ You can right click → modify <:chippedHouseTele:1127678790231916645> tabs as you see fit to teleport to other house portals
⬥ Activating the globetrotter jacket <:globetrotterjacket:1123592261175889971> during a Wilderness Crater scan will show you the level it is at, narrowing down your search route
⬥ For the North Eastern Daemonheim spot - Either use Dragonkin Laboratory Tokens <:ed2teleport:1127680553953533952> and teleport to The Shadow Reef or set your War's Retreat portal <:warsretreatteleport:1126149193908633721> to The Shadow Reef
⬥ Disassemble Dorgeshkaan Spheres <:dorgeshkaansphere:1127677637981126699> (stackable and cheap) with Scripture of Bik <:scriptureofbik:892342109791735848> equipped to receive Catalyst of Alteration <:catalystofalteration:1127677641466597537> procs
⬥ Use Lantadyme Incense sticks <:Lantadymeincense:1127691837197525033> if you'd like to extend your Spirit Attraction Potion <:spiritattractionflask:1102531518741622854> timer from 15 to 17 minutes.
⬥ If you run out of Nature Sentinel <:Naturessentinelhelm:1127691838996873366> charges, swap to the BIS Preset (No Sentinel)
⬥ For the "No Sceptre of the Gods w/o Skips" preset, use <:shadowsgrace:895999229737185280> and run the Lumbridge Swamp Cave scan in the dark if you have too.
