## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
.
This guide is tailored for players aiming to optimize their presets for solving medium clues based on their preferred style. It assumes that you have all the necessary items and offers supplementary information in specific sections to further assist you. However, if your primary focus is solely on presets, you can simply scroll to the bottom.

We welcome any suggestions for improving this guide; please share your ideas with us in the <#1060257606217773057> channel.
Should you require advice or have any questions, do not hesitate to make use of the <#400499160094212097> channel.

        ⬩ For gathering medium clues check out <#1023016130077532300>.
