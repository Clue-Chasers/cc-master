### **[Elite Fremennik](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Elite_Fremennik_achievements>)**
.
⬥ Unlimited teleports to the [Rellekka marketplace](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Rellekka>) on [Fremmenik Sea Boots](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Fremennik_sea_boots_4>) <:seaboots:1123596356175548506> and [Enchanted Lyre](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Enchanted_lyre>) <:lyre:1151959953729409034>
⬥ [Fremennik Isles](<https://runescape.wiki/w/The_Fremennik_Isles>) on the [Enchanted Lyre](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Enchanted_lyre>) <:lyre:1151959953729409034>
⬥ Ability to toggle the lyre teleport destination to Jatizso, Neitiznot, Miscellania or [Etceteria](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Etceteria>) by speaking to Olaf the Bard
### **[Hard Tirannwn](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Hard_Tirannwn_achievements>)**
⬥ No longer fail stick traps using the [Tirannwn Quiver 3](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Tirannwn_quiver_3>) <:quiver3:1123599463227867267>
⬥ Additional unlimited teleport locations: [Elf Camp](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Elf_Camp>) and Tirannwn mushroom patch

### **[Full Globetrotter outfit](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Globetrotter_outfit>)** <:globetrotterjacket:1123592261175889971>
⬥ The globetrotter outfit is an outfit that is obtainable from the [Treasure Trails Reward Shop](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Treasure_Trails_Reward_Shop>) with points obtained by completing [Treasure Trails](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Treasure_Trails>)
        ⬩ The outfit will provide several QoL features some of which are charged-based through solving clues
.
### **[Double Surge ability](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Double_Surge_codex>)** <:surgecodex:602561894414417944>
⬥ An item that can be read to unlock an upgrade to the [Surge](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Surge>) ability, giving it an extra charge with a separate independent and hidden cooldown
        ⬩ The double Surge ability is not functional in the [Wilderness](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Wilderness>) when opted into PvP
        ⬩ It can be created with 750 [codex pages](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Codex_page>) using the lectern. It is consumed upon use
        ⬩ This version can be bought from or sold to other players or the Grand Exchange, the __untradeable version only requires 500 codex pages__
### **[Bladed Dive ability](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Bladed_Dive>)** <:bd:535532854281764884>
### **[Totem of Treasure](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Totem_of_Treasure>)** <:totemoftreasure:1124764867237269665>

### **[Total level 500 in Temple Trekking](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Temple_Trekking>)** <:gamesnecklace:1124762416597700618>
⬥ Unlocks a shortcut to the [Nature’s Grotto](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Nature_Grotto#:~:text=A%20shortcut%20from%20the%20gate,The%20entrance%20to%20the%20Grotto.>) (total level 100) + [Burgh de Rott](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Burgh_de_Rott>) teleport on games necklace
.
### **[Amulet of Nature](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Amulet_of_nature>)** <:amuletofnature:1123599470911815750>
⬥ [Amulet of Nature](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Amulet_of_nature>) allows the player to teleport directly to the attended patch regardless of it's state (diseased, dead or even empty)
        ⬩ For elite clues it's recommended to bind the amulet to the [Tree patch](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Tree_patch>) in [Falador Park](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Falador_Park>)
.
### **[Sceptre of the Gods](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Sceptre_of_the_gods>)** <:sotg:1123599971023847485>
⬥ The Sceptre of the gods is an item that may be received when playing [Pyramid Plunder](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Pyramid_Plunder>)
⬥ It teleports to the same locations as the regular [pharaoh's sceptre](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Pharaohs_sceptre>) <:pharaohsceptre:1123599968477909052> but has an additional teleport to the Golden Palace in Menaphos
⬥ The sceptre has 10 teleport charges when fully charged, which is increased to 20 with [medium Desert achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Desert_achievements#Medium_achievements>) completed, and further to 60 with [hard Desert achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Hard_Desert_achievements>) completed
