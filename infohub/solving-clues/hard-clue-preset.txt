**Hard Clue Preset Guide**

> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
This guide is aimed at players looking to solve hard clues with the most optimal presets available. It assumes you have all the necessary items required. For a general understanding check out <#1120274004222161006>
To see the cost analysis of no skips vs. using skips, use the command `!casket` in <#333343575737892864>.
