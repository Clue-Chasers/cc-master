> ### **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
.
This guide is designed for players aiming to optimize their presets for solving hard clues by style. It assumes you have all necessary items and offers additional information in select sections. If you're focused solely on presets, please scroll to the bottom.

For a general understanding check out <#1120274004222161006>
To compare the cost analysis of using skips versus not using skips, use the command, `!casket` in <#333343575737892864>.
