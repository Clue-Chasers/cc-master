### Introduction (courtesy of <@145884917627224065> & <@252534317413040138>)
This guide provides a magic method for afking abyssal beasts and lords for sealed elite clues <:Elite:633732493228638209> and triskelion key <:trisk:643215216934518815> drops. Elite clues <:Elite:633732493228638209> take considerably more time to gather and solve but yield the highest chance of giving a master clue scroll <:Master:1019920500807450666> at rate of 26/100 and have a Fortunate component <:fortunatecomp:583435543845797891> rate of 44/100, note that this rate does include re-rolls and tier 4 luck. <:lotd:566453486913323042>
### Requirements
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> 115 Slayer for Abyssal Lords
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> 105 Slayer for Abyssal Beasts

### __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ Abyssal Lords and Beasts can be fought in Level 47 Wilderness:
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/AL2LhIQoXZ.png
⬥ Use a <:wildernesssword:1009419838764109834> and teleport to the Wilderness herb patch.
    • Position yourself on the westernmost corner of the rock and walk 2 tiles north and 1 tile west.
    • Place <:cannon:869283142350041098> 1 tile west of the bottom skeleton.
    • If using an alt, position yourself as shown [here](<https://img.pvme.io/images/nmGp4fMne5.png>).
⬥ Abyssal lords and beasts can be fought simultaneously at this location.
    • Abyssal lords are estimated 44.56% of kills and abyssal beasts are estimated 55.44% of kills.
.
### __Method: AFK Magic__
Before loading your <:cannon:869283142350041098> and starting the session, check the following:
__**General notes**__
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:weppoison:689525476158472288> and <:spiritattractionflask:1102531518741622854> are active at all times.
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:ectoplasmator:1023152065431744542> and <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>, <:affliction:513190158468907008> and <:protectitem:1010486261682810891>
⬥ Ensure that <:scriptureofbik:892342109791735848> is active
⬥ Autocast <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> and upkeep <:animatedead:856635090453135382>
__**Additional notes**__
- Infernal puzzle box <:infernalpuzzlebox:994189299681607800> for 14% damage reduction and 9% increase damage dealt.
- The Scavenging <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> perk is particularly useful here, as it also triggers on tentacles.
- The Stone of Jas <:jasboost:1006121672790773770> buff does not work because the player's position is out of range.
- Optional: Fill XP Capacitors <:xpcapacitor:1039869645768884256> for extra passive GP <:coins:698816156961603654>
.
