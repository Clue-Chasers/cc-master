> __**Introduction**__
Elite clues are the second highest tier clue scroll consisting of Compass and Scan steps with potential Knot and Slider puzzles. An elite clue can be between 5 to 7 steps long (4 to 6 with the Toten of Treasure).

Each Elite Casket is worth: *(Could these value be auto updated based on prices set elsewhere?)*
~ 4,690,000 gp (without skipping tickets)
~ 4,210,321 gp (using only Knot skipping tickets)
~ -1,268,909 gp (using both Knot and Puzzle skipping tickets)

You should aim to be doing up towards 20 elite clues an hour (without skipping tickets) once you have the correct set up and methods in place.

It is **__highly recommended__** to do the following before starting elite clues:
⬥ Learn how to use Alt1 effectively for Compass steps
⬥ Familiarise yourself with how scan clues work: Pulse speed and sound, Meerkat range, Recommended scan routes

__**Notes, Tips, and tricks**__
Use Lunar Spellbook with Spellbook Swap for teleports
Compass Traingulation: Moonclan (Lunars) + South Feldip Hills (Standard). A Spirit Tree is located at Feldip Hills if needed.
Disassemble Dorgesh-kaan teleport spheres for Bik procs
Dragon trinkets can be used for Brutal Greens Dragons (compass), Blue Dragons (Taverly Scan), Red Dragons (Brimhaven Scan), Black Dragons (Zanaris Scan)
Slayer mask priority - Aquanite > Kurask > Basilisk
Pollnivneach Chipped House tablets and Slayer Mask can be replaced with Skip Tickets if preferred
Activating Jacket on a Wilderness Crater scan will show the Wilderness Level, giving you a better idea of which spot it may be
Use HSR/LotD/Amulet of Glory to teleport out of Level 20-30 Wilderness
NE Daemonheim spot - Either use ED2 tablet + Chest teleport to ED3 OR have an ED3 Reaper Task and use the reaper portal
If looking to save Sentinel charges, you can modify the chipped House teleports on the fly for spots west of Rimmington - Modify → 1 → 3. Revert back when needed.
If out of Sentinel charges - Replace with Master Archaeology outfit and exchange Archaeology Teleports for Big Book o'Piracy. Jacket Fremennik Isles and do Mos Le'Harmless scan.
Use Lantadyme incense sticks to extend Spirit Attraction Potion timer and drink 1 dose prior to starting the run.
Use Spiritual Healing after summoning meerkat to extend the timer
