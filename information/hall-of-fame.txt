# Clue Records Information & Rules
.tag:info
We also track clue-solving times across various tiers, with dedicated leaderboards for each type of clue (e.g. easy, medium, hard, elite and master clue times). These leaderboards showcase the top solvers and their fastest completion times, allowing you to compete for the top spot and see how you stack up against the community!

For future updates to the leaderboard, feel free to post any eligible entries in ⁠<#1183111377955602603> and tag a <@&1376278290242732112>.
.
**Information & Rules:**
1. All submissions must be in video format. YouTube & Twitch highlights only.
2. Single clues cannot use any outfit charges but can use skips.
3. You cannot buy any outfit charges of any kind in the middle of a run.
4. The same people can hold multiple records but not in the same category.
(You can hold skip and no skip 1 but not skip 1 and skip 2). You can submit any rank however.
5. Submissions that use unobtainable or limited-access items will not be accepted. This includes the use of DTDs, as we believe they go against the spirit of speedrunning.
6. Anyone found to be abusing any form of bug within a submission will have all submissions invalidated and will be banned from the leaderboard.
7. No Skip records cannot use any type of skips. Skip records can use all. If you only opt to use puzzle skips and not knot skips it will still be a skip record and so forth.
8. When submitting a run if it's a long video please either give us the timestamps or edit the video as this greatly speeds up the verification process.
9. Boon of Scabaras procs are not permitted in single clue categories but are allowed in all other cases.
